EDUCATIONAL SUPPORT TO THE STUDENTS AFFECTED BY HIV/AIDS

We all live in communities where we all can’t excess all the personal needs as it is supposed to be even when those needs are essential in our routine life for example water, food, shelter, even when the mentioned human needs are in place but in the modern world,

students need to acquire education. In partnership with Hand in Hand for Development (HIHD), IMHH has made it possible or easier for students to have education at the highest standards. In this context the IMHH has been able to help students from families affected by HIV /AID’s by providing them with solar lamps in 2020 to help them read and revise their books while at home since their families are unable to meet the costs of hydro electricity.

This has improved the education life to paramount in these families and the students in particular giving hope to a bright future via acquiring education with the help of IMHH.

There has been a great positive change in the education life/journey of these students after the impact of IMHH to them compared to the old times in terms of performance and standards of living in the same families.
