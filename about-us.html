Welcome to I.M.H.H

IMHH is a local non- Governmental Organization founded by the people who are passionate of the persons suffering of HIV positive, Sexual Minorities, and vulnerable families. It was created with an overall objective of empowering and supporting socio-economic activities of the most vulnerable and marginalized communities. IMHH stands for Inclusive Mission for Health and Hope. Its philosophy is built on three key words: INCLUSIVE: Where we believe that every person without any kind of discrimination is a God’s creation who deserves respect and care. HOPE: Mean those who have been marginalized for long time, IMHH gives them hope and care to their life (Sexual Minorities & HIV/AIDs positive persons), and HEALTH: Every human being deserves good health conditions.
